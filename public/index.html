Kreeative Minds

If you want to search the next book that you would like to read, you have landed up on a perfect place. Kreaative Minds helps the readers to find a book by various factors. You can easily search the books by a title name, author name or genre. Also, it helps you the save the books on your bookshelf so that you can save your favorite books. You can also search for the other readers throughout the system. This is a new feature that gives you various details regarding the other readers. It also shows you the bookshelf of the other readers for you to inspire reading more books!
